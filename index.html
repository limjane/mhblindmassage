Our Story我们的故事

Dignity you can support. 用双手证明自己

M H Blind Massage Center (巧手堂) is run by members of the blind community who have turned an extraordinary sense of touch into a professional craft. Each therapist has completed rigorous training in traditional Chinese tuina, acupressure and reflexology. 巧手堂盲人按摩推拿中心由视障朋友亲手经营。他们将异于常人的敏锐触觉，磨炼成一门专业手艺。每一位按摩师都接受过严格的传统推拿、穴位按压与脚底反射疗法训练。

We are not asking for charity — we are offering excellence. Every session you book is a vote for independence, dignity and self-reliance. 我们不求施舍，只凭真本事。您预约的每一节疗程，都是对自立、自强与尊严的一份肯定。
