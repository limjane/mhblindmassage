Knowledge Corner养生知识

Understand your treatment了解您的疗程

🙌What is Tuina 推拿?什么是推拿？▾
Tuina ("push and grasp") is a 2,000-year-old therapeutic massage from traditional Chinese medicine. Unlike relaxation-only massage, tuina works along meridians and muscle groups with kneading, pressing and rolling techniques to release knots, improve circulation and restore mobility. It is commonly used for neck, shoulder and back complaints. 推拿是拥有两千多年历史的中医治疗性按摩。它沿经络与肌肉群运用揉、按、滚等手法，松解结节、促进血液循环、恢复关节活动度，常用于颈肩腰背不适。
🦶How does reflexology work?足底按摩如何起作用？▾
Reflexology applies calibrated pressure to zones on the feet that correspond to organs and body systems. The pressure stimulates nerve endings — over 7,000 in each foot — promoting relaxation, better sleep and improved circulation. Blind therapists are famed for this art: their fingertips detect texture and tension changes that others miss. 足底按摩通过按压足部反射区来对应身体器官与系统。每只脚有超过七千个神经末梢，适度按压能促进放松、改善睡眠与血液循环。视障按摩师尤以此见长——他们的指尖能察觉常人忽略的细微变化。
🌿Your first visit — what to expect初次到访——流程指南▾
Arrive in comfortable clothing — no need to undress for tuina or foot massage. Tell your therapist about any pain areas, injuries or health conditions. Pressure is always adjusted to your comfort; just say "lighter" or "stronger" anytime. A session ends with warm tea and a moment to rest. 请穿着舒适的衣物前来——推拿和脚底按摩无需更衣。请告知按摩师您的疼痛部位、旧伤或健康状况。力度随时可调，只需说"轻一点"或"重一点"。疗程结束后，我们会奉上热茶，让您稍作休息。
💧Aftercare tips疗程后的保养▾
Drink plenty of warm water to help flush metabolic waste released during massage. Avoid cold showers and heavy meals for an hour. Mild soreness the next day is normal after deep work — it fades within 48 hours. For chronic tension, a session every 1–2 weeks works best. 请多喝温水，帮助身体代谢按摩后释放的废物。一小时内避免冷水澡与过饱饮食。深层按摩后次日轻微酸痛属正常现象，一般48小时内消退。慢性劳损建议每一至两周保养一次。
